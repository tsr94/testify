How to format your questions

  • Each question should be on its own line
  • Follow the format: Question text? A) Option A B) Option B C) Option C D) Option D Answer: X
  • Make sure to include all four options (A, B, C, D)
  • End each question with "Answer: " followed by the correct letter (A, B, C, or D)
  • You can paste 15-20 questions at once

Example Format

What is the capital of France? A) London B) Berlin C) Paris D) Madrid Answer: C
Which planet is known as the Red Planet? A) Venus B) Mars C) Jupiter D) Saturn Answer: B
What is 2+2? A) 3 B) 4 C) 5 D) 6 Answer: B
